Who We Are

Born from Fire,
Grown by Craft

Ember & Oak opened in 2009 with a single hearth, four tables, and a belief that the oldest cooking method — open fire — produces the most extraordinary food. Fifteen years later, that belief hasn't changed. The hearths have grown, the tables have multiplied, but the philosophy remains unchanged.

We are first and foremost a kitchen. Every decision we make — from the farms we source, to the wood we burn, to the wines we cellar — is made in service of what ends up on your plate.

"The best meal is one where the ingredients are treated with absolute honesty and nothing is hidden."

Our menus rotate weekly, sometimes daily, depending on what the farms and the season provide. We don't force a dish onto a menu because it's a bestseller; we let the season dictate the kitchen.

What We Believe

Our Values

🌱

Honest Sourcing

We partner only with farms we visit personally. Every ingredient on our menu has a named origin. We believe transparency is respect — for the farmer, the animal, the land.

🔥

Mastery of Fire

Our chefs train for months on our live-fire hearths before ever cooking for a guest. Fire is not a gimmick — it is our primary tool and the soul of every dish we produce.

♻️

Zero Waste Kitchen

We compost, preserve, ferment, and find uses for every scrap. Our stocks run for 24 hours; our bread is made from yesterday's grains. Waste is failure.

Our Journey

15 Years, One Vision

2009

The First Fire

Ember & Oak opens with a single hearth, four tables, and a six-item menu. We are fully booked within a week of opening.

2012

The Wine Cellar

Dev Sharma joins as sommelier. We begin building our cellar and introduce our first wine pairing menu — a first in Hyderabad at the time.

2015

The Pastry Kitchen

Meera Pillai joins from Paris. We build a dedicated pastry kitchen, introducing our now-famous Valrhona fondant and seasonal tart tatin.

2018

Private Dining Room

We expand into a neighbouring space, creating our oak-panelled private dining room. It becomes the go-to venue for private celebrations in the city.

2021

Tasting Menu Launch

We introduce our seven-course tasting menu — our most personal expression yet. It sells out every weekend within a month of launch.

2024

Farm Partnership

We formalise partnerships with 12 local farms and establish our on-site preservation programme — fermenting, pickling and curing through the seasons.
