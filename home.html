Our Story

Fire, Craft,
and Devotion

Ember & Oak was born from a single belief: that the oldest cooking method — open fire — produces the most extraordinary food. Our kitchen is built around two live wood-burning hearths that run from morning until last orders.

Every plate reflects our obsession with the seasons. We source from farms within 100 miles, adjusting our menu weekly as the land dictates.
